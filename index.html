Belén Michel Torino

Belén Michel Torino is a PhD candidate in economics (UdeSA) and holds master's degrees in Economics (UNLP) and in Computational Analysis and Public Policies (University of Chicago). She has worked in evaluation and monitoring of social protection and education programs at the World Bank, the international organization for migration (UN-IOM), the national administration of social security (ANSES) and the Ministry of Education of Buenos Aires City. Curently she teaches at Universidad Nacional de La Plata (UNLP) and at the Universidad de San Andrés and she works at the Early Childhood Development Innovation Fund at the Inter American Development Bank (IDB)
